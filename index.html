Refund and Cancellation Policy

This refund and cancellation policy outlines how you can cancel or seek a refund for a hosting plan or service that you have purchased through the Platform. Under this policy:

  • Cancellations for hosting plans will only be considered if the request is made within 7 days of placing the order. However, cancellation requests may not be entertained if the service has been fully provisioned and active for more than 48 hours.
  • NextGen WebHost does not accept cancellation requests for Domain Names once they are registered, as per ICANN regulations. However, you may choose to transfer the domain or let it expire.
  • In case of receipt of incorrect service allocation or technical inability to provide the advertised specs, please report to our customer service team. The request would be entertained once the technical team has verified the issue.
  • In case of any refunds approved by NextGen WebHost, it will take 7-15 working days for the refund to be processed to your original payment method.

Return Policy

We offer a full refund (Money Back Guarantee) within the first 48 hours of purchasing a Shared Hosting or VPS plan if you are unsatisfied with the server performance. If 48 hours have passed since your purchase, you will not be offered a full refund, but a pro-rated credit may be applied to your account for future use.
